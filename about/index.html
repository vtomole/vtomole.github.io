About

My name is Victory Omole. I'm a Senior at Iowa State University majoring in Computer Engineering. I participate in a couple of open source software projects, and I have gained a lot of software and hardware engineering skills from my personal projects and classes. I am a founder of QChackers, a group of amateur Quantum engineers exploring full stack Quantum computing.

I love writing interpreters and compilers. The interpreter which I am most proud of is the Cantor programming language which won a prize for the most innovative hack at HackISU 2016. I also led a team software engineers in creating C48: A programming language inspired by C and Python.
