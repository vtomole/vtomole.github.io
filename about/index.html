About

My name is Victory Omole and I am a senior at Iowa State University majoring in Computer Engineering. I participate in a couple of open source projects, and I have gained a lot of software and hardware engineering skills from my personal projects and classes. My team made qchackers, which won the hacker prize at Hack Harvard 2017.

I love writing interpreters and compilers. The interpreter which I am most proud of is the Cantor programming language which won a prize for the most innovative hack at HackISU 2016. The project which I am currently spending most of my time on is C48: A compiler for students to learn about compilers.

I want to build a universal gate based quantum processor
