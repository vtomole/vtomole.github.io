About

My name is Victory Omole. I'm a senior at Iowa State University majoring in computer engineering. I participate in a couple of open source software projects, and I've gained a lot of software and hardware engineering skills from my personal projects and classes. I'm a founder of QChackers, a group of amateur quantum engineers exploring full stack quantum computing.

I love writing interpreters and compilers. The interpreter which I am most proud of is the Cantor programming language which won a prize for the most innovative hack at HackISU 2016. I've also led a team software engineers in creating C48: A programming language inspired by C and Python.
