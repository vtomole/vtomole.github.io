About

I am Victory Omole and I am a senior at Iowa State University majoring in Computer Engineering. I participate in a couple of open source projects, and I have gained a lot of software and hardware engineering skills from my personal projects and classes. Even though I do most of my projects alone, I am team oriented. I was able to work on a Hackathon project with 3 other people using a program language and web framework that we didn’t now (Ruby on Rails); and we managed to finish the project. I’ve also worked on team where we created a photo-sharing android application.

I love writing interpreters and compilers. The interpreter which I am most proud of is the Cantor programming language which won a $20 play-station gift card for most innovative hack in the fall 2016 Iowa State University Hackathon. The project which I am currently spending most of my time on is Aubree, a framework that I am using to learn about Quantum Computing.
