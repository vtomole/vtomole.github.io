The Statesman is an example of how to define a scientific field. The Eleatic stranger and a Young Socrates have gathered around to discuss an area of science, the science of the political. The classification of Political science goes as follows:

  • Science
    • Theoretical
      • Individual contributor
      • Manager
    • Practical

The division between the theoretical and practical is akin to the division between knowledge work and manual labor. The Individual contributor is engaged in knowledge work that does not need to be passed off to others, while the Manager does. The mathematician is an indidual contributor, while the Statesman is not, so we climb down the "Manager" node on this tree of science:

  • Manager
    • Middle manager
    • Upper manager
      • Herdsman
        • Animals
        • Human beings
      • Master-Workman

The tree in the text is more detailed than the one displayed here, but for now we see that the division has been made between Middle management and Upper management. Middle management passes on the instructions from elswhere while Upper management forges and passes on their own instructions. A prophet who passes on the instructions of God to the people is middle management.

Following the leaf of the Upper Manager, we see that this is divided into the work of the Master-Workman and Herdsman; the management of non-living things and living things respectivley. Notice, we we got farther we go down the tree the more the lines beween a practical science and a theroetical science are blurried. The mathematician only needs to worry about abstract objects, but a master-builder, like for example a engineer who is desiging a robot that fills up bottles, has to be concered with action of the beings he's controlling in order to be successful. This applies to the Herdsman and with that, the Statesman as well.
